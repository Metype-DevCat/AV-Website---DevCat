Adamsville A/V Production

Here at Adamsville High School, our A/V class works on many projects to help our students learn more about technology. We work with our students with different types of projects to expand their knowledge on audio and visual technology. Many of our students have shown great improvement with technology and explored a new career opportunity.

Our class works with cameras, microphones, speakers, and much more. The students are allowed to be creative and use the different technology we provide to create their own projects.

When you first join/take A/V I, you get an introduction to what audio really is and the point of making videos.

What you do in A/V II, you start to learn how to use editing websites like adobe and many more to help further your education in video making.

When you're finally a senior/third year in A/V III, you build upon the concepts from A/V I and II to start making more professional videos.
