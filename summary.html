General Summary

A/V is an important part of everyday life. A strong foundation in the creation and evaluation of A/V projects will serve our students well, no matter what their long term goals are.

The main focus in this class is to understand, analyze, and create Audio/Video content. After our students have an understanding of the basics of audio and video, they start to work on their own projects. The students create different audio and video production that vary from content and length. We encourage them to create production based on their own interest. We find that it is important for our students to have creativity in this class, and wish for them to be comfortable and confident in their works here.

In our class, we have a variety of projects that we do. A students can be expected to do podcasts, music videos, short films, commercials, chase scenes, and remixes. With the many projects we do, our students are given an opportunity to explore many activities involving audio and video.
