How The Class Uses Technologies, and Which Ones They Use

The importance of these products and why we use them.

The reason we use these products and why their important is because it allows out students to get the feel of what it is like to use technology that people that make things like movies, commercials, and short films.

DSLR cameras

DSLR cameras is a digital camera that uses a mirror mechanism that does one of the two things, reflect light from the camera lens to allow optical viewfinder or allow light to fully pass onto the image sensor by moving the mirror out of the way.

Adobe Premiere Pro

premiere Pro is used for editing almost all types of videos. Using Adobe premiere Pro, you can import videos, audios, and graphs. A user may also edit a video that they created.

Audacity Audio Editing Software

Audacity audio editing is recorder that records live audio, records computer playback, convert tapes, records into digital recordings, and edit different types of formats of sound files.

iMovie

iMovie is a easy free to use app that allows users to edit their videos with easy access to things like; title templates, backgrounds, and transitions.

Tripods

Tripods is a three-legged portable stand that supports objects like cameras and allows a great stability if the camera tries to tip over.

Gimbals

A gimbal is a type of device that allows people to capture smooth and stable footage from a camera while the user is moving, whether that is simple walking or running.
